A Little About Our History!

During the early 2000s, Dr. Cheersworth perfected his "Jollification Protocol," a proprietary system of assessment, intervention, and sustained holly jolly cheerfulness that has since been rolled out to corporations, schools, and municipal offices across North America. While some employees reported temporary jolliness, others discovered an existential appreciation for mandatory cheer they never knew they needed.

Today, Jolly Inc. stands as the world's premier provider of professionally manufactured happiness, with a team of certified jolly specialists ready to transform any gray cubicle farm or dreary hallway into a hub of "authentic spontaneity." We believe that with enough enthusiasm and just the right amount of forced participation, anyone can learn to smile on command.
